About CodeCamp

CodeCamp is a student-led programming academy founded by passionate Computer Science students. We believe tech education should be simple, affordable, and accessible to everyone — regardless of age or background.

Whether you're just starting out, looking to level up your skills, or exploring a future in tech — our in-person and online programs are designed to meet you where you are. We focus on hands-on learning, mentorship, and building real-world projects that matter.
